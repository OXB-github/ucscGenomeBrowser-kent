Description

These tracks show the regions of T2T CHM13 v2.0 that are unique in comparison to GRCh38/hg38 and GRCh37/hg37

Display Conventions and Configuration

Standard BED track display.

Methods

Regions not in the v1_nfLO liftOver alignments (Human liftOver tracks) for GRCh38/hg38 and GRCh37/hg19 were produced
