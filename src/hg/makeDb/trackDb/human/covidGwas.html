Description

This track set shows GWAS meta-analyses from the COVID-19 Host Genetics Initiative. Two studies are represented in this track:

  • B2: hospitalized covid vs. population (3199 cases from 8 studies)
  • C2: covid vs. population (6696 cases from 18 studies)
Data underlying these tracks was obtained from Release 3 (June 2020) results provided at https://www.covid19hg.org/results.

Display Conventions

Displayed items are colored by GWAS effect: red for positive, blue for negative. The color saturation indicates statistical significance: greater than .05 pValue are brightly colored (bright red    , bright blue    ), those with less significance are paler (light red    , light blue    ).
