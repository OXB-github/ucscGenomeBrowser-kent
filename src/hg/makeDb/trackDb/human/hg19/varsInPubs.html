Description

The tracks that are listed here contain genetic variants and links to scientific publications that mention them. The Mastermind track was created by Genomenom, a company that analyzes fulltext of publications with their own software.

For additional information please click on the hyperlink of the track above.

Display conventions

By default, each variant is labeled with the nucleotide change. Hover over the feature to see more information, explained on the track details page of the particular track or when clicking onto the feature.
