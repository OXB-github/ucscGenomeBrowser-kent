Description

This track shows the alignment of the mRNA sequence in three different mRNA vaccine sequences:

      The BioNTech/Pfizer BNT-162b2 sequence as published by WHO
        The BioNTech/Pfizer BNT-162b2 sequence as sequenced by the Andrew Fire lab, Stanford University School of Medicine
          The Moderna mRNA-1273 sequence as sequenced by the Andrew Fire lab, Stanford University School of Medicine

Display Conventions and Configuration

Methods

The mRNA sequences were obtained from the MS WORD documents as mentioned in the references below.

The PSL alignment file was obtained via the UCSC genome browser blat service with parameters -t=dnax -q=rnax

Data Access

The protein encoded by the three sequences is the same as the SARS-CoV-2 S glycoprotein:
