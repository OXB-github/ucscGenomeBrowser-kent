Description

This track shows annotations made via a public spreadsheet available at http://bit.ly/cov2annots.

Anyone can add annotations on this spreadsheet. If the sheet gets too full, we may put parts of it into separate tracks.

Display Conventions and Configuration

Only start-end annotations can be shown. Contact us if you need exons or intron lines.
