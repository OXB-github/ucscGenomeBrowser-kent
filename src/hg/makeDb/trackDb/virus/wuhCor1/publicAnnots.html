Description

This track shows annotations made via a public spreadsheet available at http://bit.ly/cov2annots.

Anyone can add annotations to this spreadsheet. Once you have added a new annotation, the changes do not go live immediately, but are screened and automatically released on the public site during during the next night after the addition. To see the most up-to-date pre-release version of this track, click this link, which will attach a track hub to your current browser view.

Display Conventions and Configuration

Only start-end annotations can be shown. Contact us if you have feedback on the form, e.g. you need exon or intron lines or have datasets with more than 5-10 annotations.
