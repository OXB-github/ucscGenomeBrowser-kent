Description

This track shows amino acid mutations in the SARS-CoV2 Spike protein, using community annotation hosted at github led by Emma Hodcroft at the Institute of Social and Preventive Medicine, University of Bern, Switzerland, supplemented by the Wikipeda Variants of SARS-CoV-2 page.

Display Conventions

The track items are colored based on the date when the mutation was first identified:
RedMost recent (Fall/Winter 2020-2021)
PurpleSummer 2020
BlueOldest (Winter/Spring 2020)
