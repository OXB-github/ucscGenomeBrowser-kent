Description

This track shows the locations of RNA structure predictions reported in Rangan et al. bioRxiv 2020 https://doi.org/10.1101/2020.03.27.012906 as well as recognizable matches to Rfam annotations found at https://rfam.xfam.org/search?q=coronavirus

Display Conventions and Configuration

At zoomed out positions RNA structure is indicated by dark (stem) and light (loops and single strands) green color bars. Zooming in to base level displays the prediction in the "extended dot-bracket" notation https://www.tbi.univie.ac.at/RNA/ViennaRNA/doc/html/rna_structure_notations.html
